Beds

Your bed should be your most comfortable piece of furniture. A custom bed will make it that way. Take a look at these examples for inspiration!

  • The overall height should be exactly what you want, based on the mattress you have. Most people prefer the mattress to reach somewhere near mid-thigh, but it can be as high as the top of your thigh.
  • Drawers can be placed wherever you want them under the mattress. They can be accessed from the sides or at the foot, and can have as much as 36 inches (1 meter) total travel.
  • Headboards are an option. Some people have wall-mounted headboards, others have none (to save space in the room). The headboard can be vertical or ergonomically angled like a chair's back. It can have whatever finish you prefer, including upholstery.

The Trellis Captain's Bed

Sleep high atop a sea of storage

Sleep like royalty in this functional captain's bed (shown here in full size) that sits high off of the floor, has four gloriously giant storage drawers and needs no box spring. The three trusses that make up the base create an impossibly light but very stable bed that requires no tools to assemble.
